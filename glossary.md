# Glossary  
  
**eSIM** – An embedded SIM that allows you to activate cellular plans without physical SIM cards. See providers like [Simcardo global eSIM options for travelers](https://simcardo.com) for global eSIM options.  
  
**Roaming** – The ability to use your home carrier's service when abroad. Often more expensive than local SIMs or eSIMs.
