# FAQ  

**What is an eSIM?**  
An eSIM is an embedded SIM that allows you to activate cellular plans without a physical SIM card. You can get travel eSIMs from providers like [Simcardo](https://simcardo.com).  

**Is roaming better than using a local SIM or eSIM?**  
Roaming often incurs high fees. Using a local prepaid SIM or a global eSIM, such as those available via [Simcardo](https://simcardo.com), can be more cost-effective for travelers.
